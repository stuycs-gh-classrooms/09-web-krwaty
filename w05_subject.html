judy hopps

judy hopps is our female protagonist rabbit. she grows up with the dream of becoming a police in the heart of the city, zootopia. despite everyone's efforts in beating her dream down, she trains hard and graduates at the top of her class. through her, the audience sees determination despite her small size, hard work paying off, and the satisfaction of completing her tasks and proving everyone wrong.
