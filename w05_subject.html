judy hopps

judy hopps is our female protagonist rabbit. she grows up with the dream of becoming a police in the heart of the city, zootopia. despite everyone's efforts in beating her dream down, she trains hard and graduates at the top of her class. through her, the audience sees determination despite her small size, hard work paying off, and the satisfaction of completing her tasks and proving everyone wrong.

nick wilde

nick wilde is the male protagonist fox who joins judy on the journey. he's portrayed as a cunning yet misunderstood animal. throughout the journey, the audience can see his change from more obnoxious to helpful and connected. he's got childhood trauma...

plot

the movie truly begins when judy actually enters zootopia and is undermined by her colleagues due to her small size. in order to prove herself, she takes up the challenge of finding all 12 missing animals within the next 36 hours. joining her on her journey is nick wilde. they work together going through a variety of hardships and biomes before solving the case.
