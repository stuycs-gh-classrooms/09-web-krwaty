Nick Wilde

Nicholas P. Wilde is a fox portrayed as a clever con-artist. His childhood dream of becoming apart of the local Junior Ranger Scouts was crushed when the fellow members, being all prey, ganged up and put a muzzle on him. This caused him some trouble learning to trust and work with others, leading to his current lifestyle. Throughout the movie, more of his other traits like his kindness, helpfulness, confidence, and charisma, helping Judy, becoming friends, and eventually her partner.
