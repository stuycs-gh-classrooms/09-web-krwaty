Nick Wilde

Description

Nicholas P. Wilde is a fox portrayed as a clever con-artist. His childhood dream of becoming apart of the local Junior Ranger Scouts was crushed when the fellow members, being all prey, ganged up and put a muzzle on him. This caused him some trouble learning to trust and work with others, leading to his current lifestyle. Throughout the movie, more of his other traits like his kindness, helpfulness, confidence, and charisma, helping Judy, becoming friends, and eventually her partner.

Main Moments

1. Nick's dream of joining the local junior ranger scouts when he was young plays a major part late movie. He shares his youth with Judy, showing his vulnerability for the first time in the movie.

2. Defending Judy against her supervisor when he tells her to hand over her badge. Nick argues with her supervisor saying that he gave her a task to complete in 48 hours when the rest of his squad haven't been able to solve it in two weeks. In doing this, he helps Judy keep her badge and earns them time to finish the case.

3. The division of the duo. Judy hands Nick a form to become ber partner which he fills out while she's doing the press conference. The images that show up as well as her words brings back his trauma. He tests the waters and sees that she's scared of him, causing him to throw the application on the floor and walk out.

4. Nick becomes the first fox in the ZPD becoming Judy's partner.
